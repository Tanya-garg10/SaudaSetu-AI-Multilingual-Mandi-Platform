# SaudaSetu AI Features

## Core Features

### 1. Real-time Multilingual Translation
- **Supported Languages**: 12 major Indian languages including Hindi, English, Bengali, Telugu, Marathi, Tamil, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi, Odia, and Assamese
- **Auto-detection**: Automatically detects the language of incoming messages
- **Context-aware**: Understands market-specific terminology and phrases
- **Real-time**: Instant translation during negotiations
- **Confidence scoring**: Provides translation confidence levels

### 2. AI-driven Price Discovery
- **Market Analysis**: Analyzes historical and current market data
- **Location-based**: Provides price insights specific to cities and states
- **Trend Analysis**: Identifies rising, falling, or stable market trends
- **Price Ranges**: Shows minimum, maximum, and average prices
- **Confidence Metrics**: Indicates reliability of price data
- **Historical Data**: Access to price history and trends over time
- **Comparative Analysis**: Compare prices across different locations

### 3. Intelligent Negotiation Engine
- **Fair Price Suggestions**: AI-powered recommendations for fair pricing
- **Market-based Offers**: Suggests offers based on current market conditions
- **Negotiation Patterns**: Learns from successful negotiations
- **Fairness Analysis**: Evaluates negotiation fairness for both parties
- **Optimal Pricing**: Calculates optimal prices based on quantity and market trends
- **Bulk Discounts**: Automatic suggestions for quantity-based pricing

### 4. Real-time Communication
- **WebSocket Integration**: Instant messaging during negotiations
- **Typing Indicators**: Shows when the other party is typing
- **Message Translation**: Automatic translation of messages
- **Offer Tracking**: Real-time updates on price and quantity offers
- **Status Updates**: Live negotiation status changes
- **Message History**: Complete conversation history

### 5. User Management & Authentication
- **Secure Registration**: JWT-based authentication system
- **Role-based Access**: Separate buyer and vendor roles
- **Profile Management**: Comprehensive user profiles
- **Location Services**: GPS-based location tracking
- **Verification System**: User verification for trust and safety
- **Preference Settings**: Language and notification preferences

### 6. Product Management
- **Rich Product Listings**: Detailed product information with images
- **Category System**: Organized product categories
- **Inventory Tracking**: Real-time quantity management
- **Search & Filters**: Advanced search with multiple filter options
- **Location-based Discovery**: Find products near your location
- **Price Management**: Dynamic pricing capabilities

### 7. Dashboard & Analytics
- **Buyer Dashboard**: 
  - Active negotiations tracking
  - Purchase history
  - Savings analytics
  - Market trends
  - Quick actions
- **Vendor Dashboard**:
  - Product management
  - Sales analytics
  - Revenue tracking
  - Negotiation management
  - Performance metrics

### 8. Market Intelligence
- **Price Trends**: Historical price movement analysis
- **Market Predictions**: AI-powered price forecasting
- **Seasonal Analysis**: Understanding seasonal price variations
- **Supply-Demand Insights**: Market dynamics analysis
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Price comparison across vendors
